Sesame crusted tuna

Description

Ingredients and cookware

  • Fresh ahi tuna fillet
  • Roasted sesame seeds
  • Light soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Half tbsp line juice
  • Half a cup of water
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • Rice
  • Avocado
  • Green scallions
  • Chilli crisp
  • Spicy mayo

Step by step instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce with sesame oil.
  2. Dip tuna into the soy sauce mixture.
  3. Add sesame seeds to the mixture.
  4. Sear onto a hot oiled pan for 10 seconds each side.
  5. Let it rest in the fridge for 15 minutes.
  6. Make sauce: Mix water, line juice, sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar and sesame oil.
  7. Slice the tuna and serve over rice with avocados.
  8. Top it off with chilli crisp, green scallions and spicy mayo.
  9. Enjoy!
