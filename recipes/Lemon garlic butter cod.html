Lemon garlic butter cod

Description

Ingredients and cookware

  • 2 cod fillet
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • Half tbsp of lemon zest
  • Half tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • Optional: pinch of chilli flakes

Step by step instructions

  1. Pet the cod fillets very dry with paper towels (this is important for crispy skin.)
  2. Season both side generously.
  3. Heat olive oil in a non-stick or stainless steel pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Place cod skin-side down and press gently with a spatula for the first 20 to 30 seconds.
  5. Cook undisturbed for 4 to 5 minutes until the skin is deeply golden and crispy.
  6. Flip the cod and reduce heat to medium.
  7. Add butter and garlic to the pan.
  8. Tilt the pan Slightly and spoon the melted garlic butter over the fish for 1 to 2 minutes.
  9. Add lemon zest, lemon juice, and chilli flakes (if using).
  10. Cook until cod is just opaque and flakes easily (internal temp ~ 52 to 55 Â°C).
  11. Remove from heat, sprinkle with parsley.
  12. Serve immediately with the crispy skin facing up.
