Grilled cheese burritos

Description

Indulge in the ultimate high-protein breakfast experience with these savory, meal-prep-friendly burritos that perfectly balance hearty nutrition and bold flavor. This recipe transforms lean beef mince and a dozen-and-a-half fluffy eggs into a satisfying filling, seasoned with a fragrant blend of smoked paprika, sage, and thyme. Each bite is elevated by a rich Worcestershire-tomato base and a zesty drizzle of sriracha mayonnaise, all tucked inside a soft tortilla that is pan-seared in melted cheese for a signature golden-brown, crispy finish.

Whether you are fueling up for the day or stocking your freezer for a busy week, these burritos are designed for maximum convenience without sacrificing quality. The recipe includes expert tips for achieving the lightest egg texture and a foolproof method for reheating that keeps the tortilla crunchy and the center piping hot. From the first stir of the onions to the final melt of the low-fat cheese seal, this dish is a delicious way to simplify your morning routine with a gourmet touch.

Ingredients and Cookware

  • 1 diced brown onion
  • 1.5kg extra lean beef mince
  • 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp sage, 1 tsp thyme, 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 diced garlic cloves
  • 100ml worcestershire sauce
  • 350g tomato paste
  • 14 eggs
  • 25g light butter
  • 10 warm tortillas
  • 25g sriracha mayonnaise
  • 20g low fat grated cheese

Step by step instructions

  1. To a pan on medium heat cook up the diced onion.
  2. Follow up with the extra lean beef mince.
  3. Season with 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp sage, 1 tsp thyme, and 2 tsp smoked paprika.
  4. As the mince is half done, add the diced garlic cloves.
  5. Top it up with 100ml worcestershire sauce and let it fully cook through.
  6. Add 350g tomato paste, mix it and set aside.
  7. For the best fluffy eggs, in a bowl, mix 14 eggs, 200g egg white and 1 tsp of salt.
  8. Add 25g of light butter to a pan on medium heat,then add the eggs.
  9. constantly stir and fold them gently until they are nice and fluffy.
  10. Grab 1 tortillas and evenly distribute eggs and the minced beef.
  11. Season with 25g sriracha mayonnaise and chives (optional).
  12. Now tightly roll the tortillas and repeat for the other burritos.
  13. Place 25g of low fat grated cheese on a pan on low heat.
  14. Place the burrito on top on the cheese for 2 to 4 minutes to seal them until they are golden brown and crispy.
  15. All done! Enjoy!
  16. For left over burritos, wrap them up in aluminium foil and store in the freezer
  • Reheat instructions
  1. Take a burrito out of the freezer, take off foil.
  2. Microwave for approximately 2 minutes (this step will thaw the burrito to ensure it warms up in the middle).
