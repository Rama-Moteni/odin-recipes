Buttery lobster tail

Description

Ingredients and Cookware

  • 6 lobster tails
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp parsley
  • Half tsp crushed red pepper
  • Salt and cracked black pepper

Step by step instructions

  1. Cut through the top shell and lift meat over the shell, keep attached at the base.
  2. Put a large skillet on low heat. Melt butter gently and add garlic and warm until fragrant (do not brown).
  3. Add tails into a pot (shell-side down).
  4. Spoon butter over constantly for 8 to 10 minutes until the meat is opaque and tender.
  5. Stir in lemon juice, zest, parsley and optional chilli flakes (taste the butter and adjust salt or pepper).
  6. Serve and enjoy!
