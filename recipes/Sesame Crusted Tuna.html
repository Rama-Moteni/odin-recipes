Sesame crusted tuna

Description

Elevate your dining experience with this vibrant sesame-crusted seared tuna bowl that brings the fresh, bold flavors of a high-end sushi bar straight to your kitchen. The process begins with a quick dip in savory sesame oil and soy sauce before each side of the tuna is expertly flash-seared for just ten seconds to achieve a perfect, buttery rare center. Chilled and sliced into delicate medallions, the tuna is served over a bed of fluffy rice and creamy avocado, creating a stunning contrast of temperatures and textures that feel both light and incredibly satisfying.

Every bite is tied together by a handcrafted, zesty dressing that balances tangy lime and rice vinegar with a hint of sweet sugar and deep soy notes. To finish, the dish is loaded with a dynamic trio of spicy mayo, crisp green scallions, and a kick of chili crisp for a modern, fiery edge. This recipe is a sophisticated yet surprisingly fast way to master the art of the poke-style bowl, offering a gourmet meal that looks professional and tastes even better.

Ingredients and cookware

  • Fresh ahi tuna fillet
  • Roasted sesame seeds
  • Light soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Half tbsp line juice
  • Half a cup of water
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • Rice
  • Avocado
  • Green scallions
  • Chilli crisp
  • Spicy mayo

Step by step instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce with sesame oil.
  2. Dip tuna into the soy sauce mixture.
  3. Add sesame seeds to the mixture.
  4. Sear onto a hot oiled pan for 10 seconds each side.
  5. Let it rest in the fridge for 15 minutes.
  6. Make sauce: Mix water, line juice, sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar and sesame oil.
  7. Slice the tuna and serve over rice with avocados.
  8. Top it off with chilli crisp, green scallions and spicy mayo.
  9. Enjoy!
