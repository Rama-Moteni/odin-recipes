BBQ Chicken Mac and Cheese

Description

Savor the perfect harmony of smoky and savory with this lighter take on a gourmet BBQ chicken mac and cheese. This recipe transforms lean diced chicken breast—perfectly seasoned with a fragrant blend of smoked paprika, garlic, and onion powder—into a succulent centerpiece glazed in a sweet and tangy mix of honey and BBQ sauce. By using fat-free evaporated milk and light cream cheese, the sauce achieves a remarkably velvety, indulgent consistency that clings to every macaroni noodle without the heavy calorie count of traditional versions.

The depth of flavor is further elevated by simmering the pasta in savory chicken stock and finishing the dish with a generous melt of sharp grated cheddar. This clever combination of ingredients ensures a rich, gooey texture and a bold, backyard-cookout profile that feels incredibly decadent while remaining health-conscious. It is the ultimate guilt-free comfort meal, offering a high-protein, smoky-sweet experience that is sure to become a staple in your weekly rotation.

Ingredients and cookware

  • 700g diced chicken breast (raw weight)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 25g honey
  • 85g reduced sugar or sugar free BBQ sauce
  • 320g macaroni pasta (un cooked weight)
  • 340ml fat free evaporated milk
  • 80g light cream cheese
  • 1.5 tsp chicken stock powder
  • 100g grated light cheddar cheese

Step by step instructions

  1. Start by seasoning your diced chicken breast with 1 tsp each of salt,smoked paprika, garlic and onion powder, the add 1 tsp olive oil and mix well.
  2. Cook the chicken on medium-high heat for 4 minutes or until cooked through.
  3. Once done cooking, take off the heat and add 25g of honey with 85g of BBQ sauce.
  4. Mix well to combine until the chicken is coated in the sauce.
  5. Start cooking the macaroni pasta in boiling water in accordance with package directions.
  6. While the pasta is cooking, to a pan on medium heat, add 350ml fat free evaporated milk , 80g light cream cheese, 1.5 tsp chicken stock powder, 100g light grated cheddar cheese and continue stirring until sauce is fully combined and creamy.
  7. Add the cooked and drained pasta to the mac and cheese sauce then mix until the pasta is fully coated.
  8. Divide into 4 equal servings and enjoy.
